Make a claim My TrueDNI

Need to make a claim?

If something’s gone wrong while you’re covered by TrueDNI, we’re here to help.

Claims are handled by AXA, with support from True Traveller.

Medical emergency?

If you need urgent medical help, seek treatment immediately.

If possible, contact AXA’s Emergency Assistance team as soon as you can.Emergency contact details can be found in your policy documents.

How to make a claim

1. Check your policy

Review your policy wording to confirm what’s covered. View policy documents

2. Get your details ready

You’ll need:

Policy number

Details of what happened

Receipts or supporting documents

3. Submit your claim

Claims are submitted directly through AXA’s online claims portal.

Make a claim online

4. Track your claim

AXA will review your claim and contact you if more information is needed.

What you can claim for

Depending on your cover:

Medical expenses

Hospital treatment

Emergency evacuation or repatriation

Travel disruption

Gadget or personal item claims

Gadget claims

Claiming for gadgets?

If you added gadget cover, include:

Proof of ownership

Proof of value

Any relevant incident or police reports

This helps avoid delays.

Need help?

If you’re unsure how to make a claim or need guidance:

Claims support: claims@truetraveller.com

General enquiries: support@truedni.com
