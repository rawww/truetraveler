About TrueDNI

Insurance for life on the move

TrueDNI is insurance designed for digital nomads, remote workers and long-stay travellers who live and work abroad.

Unlike standard travel insurance, TrueDNI is built for ongoing travel, with flexible monthly cover that moves with you
as your plans change.

How it Works

Choose the cover that fits how you travel

Pay monthly, with no long-term commitment

Cancel anytime if your plans change

Manage your policy online

Everything is designed to be simple, clear and easy to manage while you’re abroad.
